Chocolet Pizza

POSTED ON 15 DEC 2013 / DESSERTS

For the fig-swirl: Melt butter over medium heat in a saucepan. Add brown suger and stir to dissole. Halve all of the figs and toss in the saucpan with water and lemon juice. Cook over medium heat,stirring frequntly, until you have a chunky-jammy mixture. Add salt with one or two stirs, set aside and let cool completely.

Ice cream: In a small pot over medium heat, combine milk, and granuled suger until sugar is completely dissolved and the milk is just barely lukewarm. Whisk in egg yolk. Set mixture in the fridge and wait until the fig mixture is cooled.

Using an ice cream machine, pour liquids into the frozen basin and process according to manufacturer instructions, i.e., let spin and thicken for 20 minutes before adding mascarpone, fig jam mixture, and the nuts. Continue to procerss for +/-10 minutes. Pour semi-frozen mixture into a pyrex dish or glass tupperware. Freeze for a least two hours before serving.

  • 1 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup mascarpone
  • 1/2 tsp pink salt
  • 1lb Black Mission Figs
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2-4 tbsp water
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 4/3 granulated sugar
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup honey roasted pecans, roughly chopped
